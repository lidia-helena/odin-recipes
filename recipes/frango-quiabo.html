Frango com Quiabo

Description

This delicious traditional Brazilian Dish requires time and dilligence to cook. But the end-result is worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken tighs
  • 1/2 pound okra, cut in 2" circles
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, smashed
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken with garlic, satl and pepper
  2. Heat olive oil in large pan. Once hot, add chicken in an even layer, do not stir until brown
  3. Flip the chicken and cook until all liquid evaporates and chicken starts to brown
  4. Move chicken to one side of the pan, and add onions to the other side without stirring
  5. Once onions start to brown, mix chicken and onion and cook until all liquid evaporates
  6. Add just enough water to cover the chicken
  7. Cook in medium-high heat until all liquid evaporates
  8. Repeat process of adding water
  9. In the meantime, fry okra in a separate pan
  10. Once second water evaporates, add more water and okra
  11. Cook until liquid has thickened to taste
  12. Serve immediately
