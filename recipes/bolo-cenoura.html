Bolo de Cenoura

Description

The moist and fluffy Brazilian-version of a carrot cake

Ingredients

For the sponge

  • 1/2 cup of canola oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups of flour
  • 3 medium size carrots
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

For the frosting

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of chocolate powder
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

Sponge

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F
  2. In a blender, blend together the carrots, eggs, and canola oil
  3. Add the sugar and blend again on high for 5 minutes
  4. Transfer the mixture to a large bowl and slowly add the flour
  5. Add the baking powder and mix slowly with a spoon
  6. Bake for approximatedly 40 minutes

Frosting

  1. Mix together the butter, chocolate powder, sugar, and milk
  2. Bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring until creamy
  3. Once the sponge is done, pour the hot mixture over it
